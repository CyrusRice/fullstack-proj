Pieces

Piece Legal Move Legal Attack Special
Pawn Up 1 square Upper-Left or Upper-Right 1 square On pawns first move, can move 2 squares forward
King 1 square in all directions 1 square in all directions Can't move into/must move out of a checkmate position Castling - if rook and king in original positions, the king is moved two squares toward the rook, and the rook moves past the king to the square right next to where the king has moved.
Queen Unlimited squares in any direction Unlimited squares in any direction n/a
Knight up/down 1 left 2 up/down 1 right 2 right/left 1 up 2 right/left 1 down 2 Same as move n/a
Bishop Diagonal in any direction unlimited squares Diagonal in any direction unlimited squares n/a
Rook Straight in any direction unlimited squares Straight in any direction unlimited squares Castling - if rook and king in original positions, the king is moved two squares toward the rook, and the rook moves past the king to the square right next to where the king has moved.
